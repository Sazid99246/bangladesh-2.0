Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Turning creative ideas into impactful solutions that shape the future.

Sustainability

Building a greener tomorrow by promoting eco-friendly practices.

Infrastructure

Strengthening communities with modern, reliable, and smart systems.

Empowerment

Enabling people with skills, resources, and confidence to succeed.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Engergy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modren Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"The goal of the new Bangladesh is to restore peace, stability, and justice after years of mismanagement and corruption. The mission is to rebuild institutions, revive the economy, and ensure good governance, transparency, and accountability at all levels. The vision is a country where every citizen, young or old, has the opportunity to prosper; where democratic principles guide decision-making; where education, health, and welfare are accessible to all; and where the nation can progress at full speed toward sustainable growth and social equity."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
